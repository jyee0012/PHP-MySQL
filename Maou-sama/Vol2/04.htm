‚ÄúAs expected of my master, you intend to use them to the last bit.‚Äù

‚ÄúEven though I‚Äôm the only one that won‚Äôt do it, there certainly are Demon Lords that, based on the circumstances of things, will strategically assault a human city.‚Äù

Poking the hornet‚Äôs nest as they say was an old trick done by the Demon Lords.

Moreover, there would always be that chance wherein the Demon Lord would be killed by the many hero class humans he had provoked.

Simply put, it was a short-term last-ditch effort for when one needed a lot of DP.

 

‚ÄúThat sounds interesting, Oto-san. Let‚Äôs try it!‚Äù

‚ÄúNo, let‚Äôs not.‚Äù

 

Kuina could be kind of capricious sometimes so I decided I should be careful.

 

 

‚óá

But then, a group of three boorish men appeared and looked like they intended to stand in our way.

They were garbed in light armor with worn-out one-handed swords hanging from their sides.

 

‚ÄúYou little misses look cute. Wanna go eat some tasty food with us? We just scored some good stuff from the dungeon so we can treat you to some expensive food. Whaddaya say?

‚ÄúCould you please not bother my companions?‚Äù

‚ÄúHey you look like a girl. Anyway, what, you their friend or something? You trying to show-off? Well, we‚Äôre just kind misters who are going to show them something more fun than playing games so don‚Äôt get in the way, okay?‚Äù

 

I looked like a girl?

It bothered me, to be honest.

I then felt Kuina repeatedly poking my back.

 

‚ÄúMay I burn these scum who dared talk like that to Oto-san?‚Äù

‚ÄúI‚Äôm forbidding any killing today. Moreover, don‚Äôt get involved, okay?‚Äù

‚ÄúOkay.‚Äù

‚ÄúI‚Äôm not their friend; I‚Äôm their guardian. I have a duty to protect these girls so I will get in the way.‚Äù

‚ÄúI see‚Ä¶ go to sleep then!‚Äù

‚ÄúIt hurts! What the, hitting this guy‚Äôs like hitting iron!‚Äù

 

The man held his hand and crouched down. Well, hitting me would do that to someone like him.

The man then raised his face and upon doing so, he began to tremble. The other two men also trembled soon after.

 

‚ÄúAH! AH! AHHH!‚Äù

‚ÄúHii!‚Äù

‚ÄúW-waaaaahhhhh!‚Äù

 

The three screamed as they began their escape.

I did not cause their panic, however.

 

‚ÄúYou guys have endured well.‚Äù

 

It was caused by my monsters.

They saw that the men actually dared to hurt me but since I had forbidden them to kill, the release of their urge to kill was all they did. It was, however, enough to make the men face their doom.

In the aftermath of things, the surrounding humans also lost their marbles.

They truly were S rank monsters.

 

‚ÄúOto-san, please take back your order of not getting involved. Even though I knew it won‚Äôt hurt you at all, I still didn‚Äôt like it. I could have kill him a dozen times even before his slow fist hit.‚Äù

‚ÄúMe too, master.‚Äù

‚ÄúYeah, I agree. I won‚Äôt let anyone bring harm to my master.‚Äù

 

Being loved by my girls really made me happy.

With no other way, I decided to stop getting myself be purposely hit next time.

 

‚ÄúI‚Äôm sorry, I didn‚Äôt mean to make you guys worry. I‚Äôll properly make it up to all of you.‚Äù

 

I had decided that after our tasks for the day were finished, I would let everyone enjoy themselves to the fullest.

 

‚óá

 

We had finished the viewing of the market price of the goods so next was, in a way, the day‚Äôs main event.

 

‚ÄúFrom here on, we‚Äôre gonna be looking around mainly for weapons. Be especially mindful, Elder Dwarf. We can sell any weapons we have that are better than the ones here. Use it as reference.‚Äù

‚ÄúUnderstood but I think it‚Äôs unnecessary; it‚Äôs unlikely humans could make something great.‚Äù

‚ÄúThat‚Äôs certainly true, yeah. I also share your sentiments but we‚Äôll still use it as reference. After all, it won‚Äôt do us any good if we offer the humans weapons that are too powerful. So, it‚Äôs important to sell them only slightly better weapons than the ones sold here.‚Äù

 

For argument‚Äôs sake, let us assume we mass produced and sold assault rifles in my town.

It would be really popular and make the humans clamor over it. But this would invite the ire of the other Demon Lords and thus be like digging my own grave.

On the other hand, the far-too-advanced technology would stimulate the humans‚Äô greed and, in order to have a monopoly over it, they would wage war on me in the true sense of the word.

‚ÄúI understand. I‚Äôll closely examine the things here and make some drafts. Afterwards though, I‚Äôll leave the manufacturing to those two. Mundane tasks are so boring after all.‚Äù

‚ÄúI love you, master.‚Äù
